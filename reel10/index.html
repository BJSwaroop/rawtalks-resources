The Framework
Marks-Per-Hour ROI
Every chapter ki 2 factors undi: weightage (enni questions vasthayi) + difficulty (enduku kashtam). Eedhanni combine chesthe — ROI vasthundi.
🟢

Gold Mine Chapters

HIGH WEIGHTAGE + EASY
Eevvi nee first priority. Ekkuva questions vasthayi, inka direct formulas/memorization tho solve cheyochu. 1 hour study = 3-4 marks average ROI.
🔵

Smart Pick Chapters

HIGH WEIGHTAGE + MODERATE
Important chapters but need practice. Questions ekkuva vasthayi, but conceptual understanding kavali. 1 hour = 2 marks average ROI.
🟠

Time Sink Chapters

LOW WEIGHTAGE + HARD
Eevvi lo ekkuva time spend cheyaku. Questions thakkuva inka difficulty ekkuva. Skip or last lo chudhu. Only basics enough.
Key Insight

160 marks lo ~85 marks "Gold Mine" chapters nunchi vasthayi. Avi master chesthey — under 5000 rank guaranteed.

10-Day Gold Mine Plan
1

Day 1-2: Coordinate Geometry

Straight Lines, Circles, Conics. Practice 30 PYQs. Target: 20+ marks from Maths alone.

2

Day 3: Probability + Matrices

Formulae memorize → 20 PYQs each. Direct substitution type questions ekkuva. Target: 10 marks.

3

Day 4-5: Inorganic Chemistry

p-Block, d-Block, Periodicity, s-Block — pure memorization. NCERT tables chadhuvu. Target: 10 marks.

4

Day 6: Chem Easy Chapters

Biomolecules, Polymers, Everyday Chemistry, Environmental Chemistry, Surface Chemistry. Oka roju lo 5 chapters finish. Target: 5 marks.

5

Day 7-8: Physics Gold Mines

Units & Measurements, Current Electricity, Thermodynamics, Semiconductors, Communication. Formulae + PYQs. Target: 15 marks.

6

Day 9: Basic Concepts + Atomic Structure

Mole concept, Stoichiometry, Bohr model — fundamental topics. Target: 5 marks.

7

Day 10: Full Mock + Revision

Full-length mock attempt → only Gold Mine chapters review. Weak spots identify → last 2 hours formula revision.

Remember
