The Formula
Why 50M + 25P + 25C Works
Most students try equal marks in all 3 subjects. That's wrong. Here's why Maths should be your PRIMARY weapon.
50M + 25P + 25C
= 100 Marks = Under 5,000 Rank
EAMCET Total: 160 marks (80 questions × 2 marks each)

Why Maths gets 50?

EAMCET Maths is the MOST scoring subject. Questions are formula-based and direct. If you know the formula — you can solve in 30 seconds. No lengthy derivations like JEE. 80 marks total in Maths (40Q) — targeting 50 means getting 25/40 correct.

Why Physics gets 25?

Physics is tricky in EAMCET — conceptual + numerical. Some questions take 3-4 minutes. Target the easy chapters (Units, Electrostatics, Optics) and leave the tough ones. 25/40 marks from focused chapters.

Why Chemistry gets 25?

Chemistry has too much theory to memorize for EAMCET. But Organic Reactions + Periodic Table + Chemical Bonding can give you 25 marks easily with focused revision. Skip the low-yield topics.

Scoring Hack

EAMCET Maths lo formula-based questions dominate. Make a formula sheet (2 pages max). Revise it daily for 15 minutes. On exam day, those 15 minutes of daily revision = 10-15 extra marks guaranteed.

🧪 Chemistry

Focus Chapters (20-22 marks easy):
Chemical Bonding, Periodic Properties, Organic Reactions (Named Reactions), Thermodynamics, Electrochemistry, Solutions

Quick Wins (5-8 marks from memory):
Polymers, Biomolecules, Chemistry in Everyday Life — pure rote, read once before exam

Mechanics (8-10Q): Kinematics, Laws of Motion, Work-Energy. Formula-heavy = scoring if formulas known.

Electrodynamics (8-10Q): Electrostatics + Current Electricity + Magnetism. Highest weightage in Physics.

Optics + Modern (5-7Q): Ray Optics is easy marks. Wave Optics can be skipped if time is short.

Waves + Thermo (5-7Q): Thermodynamics is scoring. Sound waves — only basic formula questions.

Exam Day
3-Hour Battle Plan
100 marks strategy paper pe hoti hai, but execution exam hall mein. Idi follow cheyyi.

⏱️ First 90 Minutes — Maths

Start with Maths. It's your highest-target subject. Do easy questions first (formula-based). Mark tough ones and come back. Target: 25+ questions attempted, 20+ correct.

⏱️ Next 45 Minutes — Physics

Physics ke switch cheyyi. Do your strong chapters first (Electrostatics, Optics, Units). Skip lengthy numerical problems for now. Target: 15+ questions attempted.

⏱️ Last 45 Minutes — Chemistry + Revision

Chemistry theory questions first (fastest to answer). Then remaining Chemistry numericals. Last 10 minutes: go back to skipped questions in ALL subjects. ATTEMPT EVERYTHING — no negative marking!

NO NEGATIVE MARKING
EAMCET has no negative marking. Every blank answer = guaranteed 0. Every guessed answer = 25% chance of +2. NEVER LEAVE ANY QUESTION BLANK.
Time Allocation Rule

If a question takes more than 2 minutes — SKIP IT and move on. Come back in the last 10 minutes. 2 minutes per question = 80 questions in 160 minutes. You have 180 minutes total. Use the extra 20 minutes for review.
