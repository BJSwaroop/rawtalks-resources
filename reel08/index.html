Codes 1 & 2
Unit Analysis + Trig Substitution
Ivi Physics and Maths lo most powerful — question solve cheyakundane answer guess cheyyochu.
01
🔍 Unit Analysis
Works in: Physics (80% of numerical questions)

Question lo answer ki expected unit ento decide cheyyi. Then check which option has that unit. Wrong unit = wrong answer — eliminate instantly.

Example: "Find the force..." → Answer must be in Newtons (kg·m/s²).
Option A: 5 kg·m/s² ✅ (correct unit)
Option B: 5 kg·m/s ❌ (momentum, not force)
Option C: 5 J ❌ (energy, not force)
Option D: 5 kg/m³ ❌ (density, not force)
Result: You eliminated 3 wrong answers without solving!
+2-4 marks per exam
02
📐 Trig Substitution
Works in: Maths (Integration, Trig, Limits)

Integration/Trig problem lo — don't solve. Put standard values (0, π/6, π/4, π/2, π) into the options and check which one gives the right answer. Works for definite integrals and trig identities.

Example: "Find ∫₀^(π/2) sin²x dx"
Instead of solving: Put x = π/2 boundary conditions into options.
Option A: π/4 → check: does it satisfy? ✅
Saved 3 minutes of actual integration!
+4-6 marks per exam
Code 3
Strategic Guessing
EAMCET lo NO NEGATIVE MARKING. This changes EVERYTHING about how you approach the exam.
03
🎲 Strategic Guessing
Works in: ALL subjects — every single question

Simple math: Each question has 4 options. Random guess = 25% chance of getting +2 marks. Blank answer = 0% chance.

If you have 10 questions you can't solve:

• Leave blank: 0 marks guaranteed

• Guess all 10: Expected value = 2.5 correct = 5 marks

5 marks ki rank difference: 500-1000 ranks

The Math:
10 guesses × 25% accuracy = 2.5 correct answers
2.5 × 2 marks each = 5 marks free
0 risk (no negative marking) + 5 expected marks = NO-BRAINER
+4-6 marks per exam (statistically)
But Smart Guessing > Random Guessing

If you can eliminate even 1 wrong option, your guess accuracy jumps from 25% to 33%. Eliminate 2 options = 50% accuracy. Always try to eliminate before guessing. Use Unit Analysis (Code 1) to eliminate options even when you can't solve.

Codes 4 & 5
Elimination + "All of the Above"
Ivi theory-heavy Chemistry and Physics lo especially powerful.
04
✂️ Option Elimination
Works in: All subjects, especially Chemistry

When 2 options are very similar (differ by just a sign, constant, or small detail) — one of them is usually correct. The question maker created the "close wrong answer" from the right answer. Eliminate the outliers first.

Example: "The bond angle of H₂O is..."
A) 104.5° B) 109.5° C) 90° D) 180°

C and D are outliers (90° = right angle, 180° = linear). Between A and B — these are close, so answer is likely one of them.
A) 104.5° is correct. You narrowed it to 50-50 without knowing the theory!
+2-4 marks per exam
05
✅ "All of the Above" Shortcut
Works in: Chemistry theory, Biology, some Physics

If the question has "All of the above" as an option and you can verify that 2 out of 3 statements are correct — choose "All of the above". Don't waste time verifying the 3rd statement.

Logic: If 2 are correct, the answer is either "All of the above" or "Both A and B" type. But if "All of the above" is an option and 2 are verified correct — it's almost always the answer. Saves 1-2 minutes per question.
+2-3 marks per exam

How to Practice These Codes

Download any EAMCET previous year paper. First, try to solve normally. Mark the ones you can't solve.

🔓 Step 2: Apply Cheat Codes on Unsolved

Go back to every unsolved question. Try Unit Analysis first. Then Elimination. Then Trig Substitution. See how many you can crack WITHOUT actually knowing the concept.

📊 Step 3: Track Your "Cheat Code Marks"

Count how many marks these tricks gave you. After 5 papers, you'll see a consistent +10-15 marks. That's your "cheat code bonus" for the actual exam.

⚡ Step 4: Combine Codes

The real power is COMBINING tricks. Example: Use Unit Analysis to eliminate 2 options → Then Strategic Guess between remaining 2 → 50% accuracy instead of 25%. Stacking codes = maximum marks.
