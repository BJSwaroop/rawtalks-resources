Government Institutes
IIT & NIT Fee Waivers
IITs and NITs have built-in fee waiver systems. Most students don't even know about these!

🏛️ IIT Tuition Fee Waiver

Up to 100% Fee Waiver

Family income < ₹1 lakh/year: 100% tuition fee waiver — you pay ZERO tuition.

Family income ₹1L – ₹5L/year: 66.67% tuition fee waiver — you pay only 1/3 of fees.

SC/ST students: Full tuition fee waiver + additional benefits (free mess, hostel rent waiver, pocket allowance at many IITs).

AUTO APPLIED — just submit income certificate during admission.

🎓 Merit-cum-Means (MCM) Scholarship

₹1,000/month + Fee Waiver

Eligibility: Family income < ₹4.5L/year + minimum 6.0 CGPA

Coverage: 25% of all UG students at each IIT receive this every year.

How: Apply through your IIT's Academic Section after first semester results.

Mind = Blown Fact

At IITs, if your family income is under ₹1 lakh — you study for FREE. Between ₹1-5 lakh — you pay less than ₹1 lakh/year. Engineering at an IIT can cost less than a private coaching class!

NIT Tuition: ₹63,500 – ₹1.5L per semester (varies by NIT). SC/ST: Often fully waived.

IIITs: ₹1.5L – 2L per year tuition. Some IIITs have industry-funded scholarships.

Post-Matric Scholarship: SC/ST/OBC students at NITs can apply through state post-matric scholarship portals for additional fee reimbursement.

Central Government
Government Scholarship Schemes
Central government ki separate scholarship schemes unnayi — ivanni NSP (National Scholarship Portal) through apply cheyyaali.

📘 CSSS — Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship

₹12,000/year (1st-3rd year) → ₹20,000 (4th year)

Eligibility:

• Above 80th percentile in Class 12 board exam

• Family income ≤ ₹4.5 lakh/year

• Pursuing regular degree (not distance/correspondence)

• Not receiving any other central/state scholarship

Apply: scholarships.gov.in (NSP Portal)

🏦 Interest Subsidy on Education Loans

0% Interest During Study Period

How it works: Government pays the interest on your education loan during your course + 1 year after (moratorium period).

Eligibility: EWS families, loans up to ₹7.5L from nationalized banks.

Impact: On a ₹5L loan at 8.5% interest, this saves you ₹1.7L+ in interest!

⚠️ Common Mistake

INSPIRE Scholarship is NOT for engineering! Many websites list INSPIRE (₹80K/year) but it's only for Basic/Natural Sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Math, Biology) — NOT for B.Tech/B.E. students. Don't waste time applying for it.

Post-Matric Scholarship (SC/ST)

Covers: full tuition + maintenance allowance (₹3K-15K/month depending on course). Apply through state social welfare department. Available in all states.

OBC Post-Matric Scholarship

For OBC-NCL students with family income < ₹2.5L. Covers partial tuition. Apply via NSP or state backward class department.

Minority Scholarships

Pre-matric and post-matric scholarships for minority community students. Apply through NSP. Income limit varies by scheme.

Private Colleges
College-Specific Scholarships
Top private colleges offer massive merit scholarships. Some cover 100% fees — based on entrance exam rank.

🅱️ BITS Pilani

Up to 100% Tuition Waiver

Top 500 BITSAT rankers: Full 4-year tuition scholarship (family income < ₹20L/year + min 7.5 CGPA).

25% of all admitted students: Get 10-100% semester fee waiver based on merit and need.

🎯 VIT Vellore

Up to 100% Fee Waiver

VITEEE Rank 1-10: 100% tuition fee waiver for all 4 years.

Rank 11-50: 75% fee reimbursement.

Also has GVDSP scholarships for state/central board toppers.

🏫 SRM University

Up to 75-100% Scholarship

SRMJEEE toppers get up to 100% tuition waiver. Board exam toppers also eligible. Scholarship renewed yearly based on CGPA.

Manipal (MIT): Merit scholarships based on MET rank. Top rankers get significant fee reductions. Also offers need-based financial aid.

LPU: NEST scholarship exam — up to 100% fee waiver for toppers.

Amity: Merit-based scholarships for JEE qualified students. 25-100% fee waiver.

Thapar: Institutional scholarships for top CGPA holders each semester.

Pro tip: Always ask the admissions office about scholarships — many colleges have unpublicized need-based aid!

Strategy

Stack multiple scholarships! You can get IIT fee waiver + MCM + CSSS simultaneously. A student from a ₹3L income family at IIT could be getting paid to study instead of paying fees.

Action Plan
How to Apply — Step by Step
Scholarship apply cheyyakundaa money raadu. Idi exact process.
  1. Create NSP Account NOW Go to scholarships.gov.in. Register with Aadhaar. This is the portal for ALL central government scholarships including CSSS.
  2. Get Income Certificate Ready From Tahsildar/MRO office. Most scholarships need family income proof. Apply early — sometimes takes 2-4 weeks to process.
  3. Apply to College-Level Scholarships During Admission IIT fee waivers are applied during admission itself — submit income certificate. MCM is applied after first semester. ASK your admission office about all available scholarships.
  4. Apply on NSP Within the Window NSP scholarship windows usually open Aug-Nov each year. CSSS deadline is typically October-November. Set reminders!
  5. Check State Government Portals AP: jnanabhumi.ap.gov.in | TS: telanganaepass.cgg.gov.in. State scholarships are SEPARATE from central. Apply to both!
Documents Checklist

✅ Aadhaar Card (linked to bank account)
✅ Income Certificate (from Tahsildar)
✅ Caste Certificate (if applicable)
✅ 10th & 12th Marksheets
✅ College Admission Letter/ID
✅ Bank Account (in YOUR name — not parent's)

Get ALL of these ready BEFORE college starts!
