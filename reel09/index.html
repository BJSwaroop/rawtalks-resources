The System
Swaroop's Category System
All 80 questions are NOT equal. Some chapters give 3-4 questions (8 marks), some give just 1 (2 marks). Idi naa categorization:
C1

Category 1: MUST Score

High weightage + easy to score. 60-70% of your marks come from here. Prepare these FIRST and THOROUGHLY.
C2

Category 2: SHOULD Score

Medium weightage. Worth studying if time permits. 20-25% of marks. Study after completing all C1 chapters.
C3

Category 3: SKIP or Guess

Low weightage, high effort. Only 1 question max. Use cheat codes (elimination, guessing) for these. Don't waste study time here.
The Rule

Never study a C3 chapter before completing ALL C1 chapters. If you have 10 days left — spend 7 days on C1, 2 days on C2, and 1 day for revision. C3 = exam day guessing only.

Exam Strategy
Paper Attempt Order
Which subject first? Which questions first? Idi follow cheyyi — time waste avoid cheyyi.

Phase 1: Maths First (90 min)

Start with Maths — it's your highest-target subject. Scan ALL 80 questions. Do C1 chapters first, then C2. Mark C3 for later guessing.

Phase 2: Physics (45 min)

Switch to Physics. C1 chapters first — Laws of Motion, Current Electricity, Kinematics. Skip any question taking >2 min.

Phase 3: Chemistry (30 min)

Chemistry theory questions = fastest to answer. Read and mark. Named reactions, periodic trends, bonding — these are memory-based = quick.

Phase 4: Mop-up (15 min)

Go back to ALL skipped questions. Apply cheat codes. GUESS everything remaining. No question should be blank!

The 2-Minute Rule

If a question takes more than 2 minutes and you're stuck — SKIP IT IMMEDIATELY. Mark it and move on. Come back in Phase 4. Spending 5 minutes on 1 tough question = losing 2 easy questions elsewhere. Time management > knowledge.
