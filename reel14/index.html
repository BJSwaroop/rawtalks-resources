The Battle Plan
Days 1-5: Build
First 5 days = high-weightage chapters revise + PYQs solve. No new topics — only strengthen what you know.
D1

Coordinate Geometry + Formulae

Target: 20+ marks from Maths
  • Straight Lines — all formulae + 10 PYQs
  • Circles — standard forms + 10 PYQs
  • Conics — Parabola, Ellipse basic forms + 5 PYQs each
  • Night: Write all formulae in a separate notebook
D2

Probability + Vectors + Matrices

Target: 15 marks (formulaic chapters)
  • Probability — Bayes theorem, distributions + 10 PYQs
  • Vector Algebra — dot/cross product + 8 PYQs
  • Matrices — inverse, adjoint + 5 PYQs
D3

Inorganic Chemistry Blitz

Target: 10 marks (pure memorization)
  • p-Block — Group 13-18 properties, NCERT tables
  • d & f Block — transition elements, properties
  • Periodicity — trends, exceptions
  • Night: Biomolecules, Polymers, Everyday Chemistry (3 easy chapters = 3 marks)
D4

Physics Gold Mines

Target: 15 marks
  • Units & Measurements — dimensional analysis + 5 PYQs
  • Current Electricity — Kirchhoff's, Wheatstone + 8 PYQs
  • Thermodynamics — laws, PV diagrams + 8 PYQs
  • Semiconductors — p-n junction, transistor basics
D5

Organic Chemistry + Trig

Target: 12 marks
  • Organic reactions — named reactions list revise + 10 PYQs
  • GOC — mechanisms, isomerism + 5 PYQs
  • Trigonometry — formulae sheet + 10 PYQs
  • Night: All Chemistry formulae in formula notebook

D-Day
Exam Day Battle Plan
3 hours, 160 questions. Time management is everything. Follow this exact order.
⏱️ MATHS (90 min) → PHYSICS (45 min) → CHEMISTRY (30 min) → MOP-UP (15 min)

📐 Phase 1: Maths First (90 min)

Why first: 80 questions = 50% marks. Fresh mind lo attempt cheyyi.

Order: Coordinate Geometry → Probability → Vectors → Matrices → rest

Rule: 1 min per question. Stuck ayithey skip → mark for later.

⚡ Phase 2: Physics (45 min)

Order: Units & Measurements → Current E → Thermodynamics → Mechanics

Easy ones first: Theory-based questions attempt cheyyi, then numericals.

Rule: No negative marking — attempt everything!

🧪 Phase 3: Chemistry (30 min)

Order: Inorganic (memorization) → Organic (reactions) → Physical

Speed focus: Inorganic questions lo 30 seconds enough — pick answer from memory.

🎯 Phase 4: Mop-up (15 min)

Go back: to skipped questions. Try elimination method.

Guess remaining: No negative marking — NEVER leave blank!

Remember: Random guess = 25% chance correct = free marks!

Critical Rule

EAMCET lo negative marking LEDU. Blank answer = 0 marks guaranteed. Random guess = 25% chance of +1. Always mark something!
